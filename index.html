Founder

Johnnita Bros

I’ve spent over a decade at Fortune 500 companies, managing budgets and analyzing the numbers behind major business decisions.

Now, I help coffee shop and bakery owners bring that same level of clarity to their own finances through simple tools, clear reporting, and practical guidance you can actually use.

  • Why

    Your shop can be busy all day and still not feel profitable. Drinks are going out, pastries are selling, but the cash never quite lines up. Without clear numbers, it is hard to see what is working and where money is slipping away.

  • Who

    You are running a coffee shop or bakery. You are managing staff, inventory, waste, and a steady flow of customers. There is no time to sort through reports just to answer one question: is this actually making money?

  • Approach

    Focus on what matters each week: sales, labor, and ingredient costs. No complicated systems. No unnecessary reports. Just clear numbers you can check quickly and use to make better decisions.

  • Next

    Start with a simple tool. Track your numbers weekly and get a clear view of where your money is going. Once you understand your numbers, it becomes easier to spot what needs attention: costs, pricing, or labor. From there, you can go deeper with clearer reporting, ongoing support, and a more structured approach as your shop grows.
